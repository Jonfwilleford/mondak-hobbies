How It Works

What Is a Box Break?

A box break is a group opening of a sealed hobby box where each participant owns a slot — typically a team, division, or random pick. We open it live on stream so you can watch every pack in real time. Any cards pulled for your slot get shipped directly to you.

02

Join the stream when the break goes live. Every pack gets opened on camera — no surprises, no tricks.

03

Receive Your Cards

We securely sleeve, top-load, and ship every card from your slot straight to your door.
