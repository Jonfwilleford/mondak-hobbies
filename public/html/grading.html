Why Grade?

What Is PSA Grading?

PSA (Professional Sports Authenticator) is the world's leading third-party grading company. A graded card comes encased in a tamper-evident slab with a numeric grade from 1–10. A PSA 10 can be worth multiples of an ungraded raw card — grading protects and legitimizes your investment.

Increased Value

A PSA 9 or 10 grade can dramatically increase a card's market value — sometimes 5–10x over a raw copy.

Verified Authenticity

Every card is examined by PSA experts. Grading confirms your card is genuine and not altered or counterfeit.

Protected Forever

Graded cards live in a rigid, UV-resistant slab — safer than a sleeve and ready to display or resell.

Service Tiers

Submission Levels

PSA offers multiple service levels based on card value and turnaround time. We submit at any level — contact us to discuss which tier makes the most sense for your cards.

Value
Cards valued under $500. Best for raw rookies and commons you want graded affordably.
Regular
Cards valued up to $1,499. Faster turnaround for mid-range singles worth protecting.
Express
Faster turnaround for valuable cards. Great for new releases you want graded quickly.
Super Express
Priority processing for high-value cards. Quickest path to a slab for your best hits.
